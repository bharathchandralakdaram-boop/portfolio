My Hobbies

Life isn't just about code. Here's what keeps me inspired, balanced, and creative.

📖
Reading

From science fiction to philosophy and tech blogs, reading fuels my curiosity. I love exploring ideas across domains — it keeps my mind sharp and creative.

🎸
Music

Music is my stress buster. I enjoy listening to a wide range of genres and have been casually learning to play the guitar in my spare time.

🎮
Gaming

Strategy and puzzle games sharpen logical thinking. I enjoy both single-player story games and occasional competitive gaming sessions.

📸
Photography

I love capturing moments — street photography, nature, and architecture. My phone camera is always ready. It trains my eye for visual design too.

🏃
Running

Morning runs clear my mind and boost productivity. I aim for consistency over speed, and running has become a meditative habit I look forward to.

🧩
Problem Solving

Solving puzzles, riddles, and competitive programming challenges is a hobby that directly strengthens my coding skills. I practice on LeetCode regularly.

✈️
Travelling

Exploring new places, cultures, and perspectives is deeply enriching. Travel teaches empathy and broadens the way I think and create.

🎨
UI/UX Design

Beyond code, I enjoy sketching UI ideas and exploring design systems. Great design is invisible — I love the craft of making interfaces feel natural.

📝
Blogging

Writing helps me consolidate what I learn. I occasionally write about web development tips, tools, and beginner guides on platforms like Dev.to.
